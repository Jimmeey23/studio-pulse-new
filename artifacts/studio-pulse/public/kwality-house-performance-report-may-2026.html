01 · Executive Summary

May closed the April anomaly cleanly — the studio is back to baseline-plus on volume, while discount discipline is the new priority.

Headline revenue normalised to ₹23.13L net, which is +9.0% above the Jan – Mar baseline and confirms the underlying business is healthier than the bare MoM -51.6% suggests.
